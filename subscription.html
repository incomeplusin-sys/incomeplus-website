Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our subscription plans

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens if I exceed my monthly scan limit?
If you exceed your monthly scan limit, you can purchase additional scans or upgrade to a higher plan. Basic users can buy scan packs, while Pro and Enterprise users get notifications when approaching their limits.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for all new users. You get access to all Pro plan features during the trial period. No credit card required to start the trial.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of your subscription, contact our support team for a full refund.
