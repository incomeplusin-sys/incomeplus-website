Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2024

VIOLATION OF THIS POLICY MAY RESULT IN:

  • Immediate account termination
  • Legal action under Indian IT Act 2000
  • Reporting to law enforcement agencies
  • Civil and criminal liability

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy defines the proper and legal use of IncomePlus security scanning services. Our platform is designed for ethical security testing and educational purposes only.

2. Authorized Use

You may use IncomePlus ONLY for:

  • Testing systems you own or have explicit written permission to test
  • Educational purposes in security training and courses
  • Bug bounty programs where you have authorization
  • Compliance verification for regulatory requirements
  • Security research with proper authorization

3. Prohibited Targets (STRICTLY FORBIDDEN)

You MUST NOT scan the following under ANY circumstances:

  • Government domains: .gov.in, .nic.in, .gov., .mil., .police.
  • Military domains: .mil.in, .army., .navy., .airforce.
  • Judiciary domains: .judiciary., .court., .highcourt.
  • Private networks: 192.168.*, 10.*, 172.16-31.*
  • Localhost/loopback: 127.0.0.1, localhost
  • Critical infrastructure: Banking, healthcare, utilities
  • Systems you don't own without written permission

4. Prohibited Activities (ILLEGAL)

The following activities are STRICTLY PROHIBITED and may be reported to law enforcement:

  • Unauthorized access: Section 43, IT Act 2000
  • Data theft: Section 66, IT Act 2000
  • Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS) attacks
  • Brute force attacks or credential stuffing
  • Exploitation of vulnerabilities for malicious purposes
  • Phishing or social engineering attacks
  • Malware distribution or command & control
  • Scanning for criminal activity

5. Compliance Requirements

All users must comply with:

  • Indian IT Act 2000 and amendments
  • SEBI Cybersecurity Guidelines for financial sector
  • RBI Payment Security Standards
  • DPDPA 2023 for data protection
  • Copyright Act 1957
  • All applicable Indian laws

6. Documentation Requirements

For compliance audits, you must maintain:

  • Written authorization for all targets scanned
  • Scope documentation defining test boundaries
  • Contact information for target owners
  • Test results and vulnerability reports
  • Remediation evidence for findings

7. Enforcement & Consequences

IncomePlus will:

  • Monitor all scans for policy violations
  • Immediately terminate violating accounts
  • Report violations to Indian law enforcement (CERT-In, Cyber Crime Cells)
  • Preserve evidence for legal proceedings
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

8. Ethical Hacking Guidelines

For ethical hackers, follow these principles:

  • Always get permission in writing
  • Define scope clearly before testing
  • Do no harm to systems or data
  • Respect privacy and confidentiality
  • Report findings responsibly to authorized contacts
  • Delete sensitive data after testing

  • You will comply with all provisions
  • You accept legal liability for violations
  • All scans are logged and may be used as evidence
