Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Email, name, company details
  • Usage Data: Scan history, IP addresses, browser information
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Razorpay
  • Communication Data: Support requests and feedback

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our security scanning services
  • Processing payments and subscriptions
  • Ensuring compliance with Indian IT Act 2000
  • Improving our platform and developing new features
  • Communicating important updates and security alerts

3. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Free Users: Scan data retained for 30 days
  • Paid Users: Scan data retained for 1 year
  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Compliance Logs: Retained for 5 years as required by law

4. Your Rights (DPDPA 2023 Compliant)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent
  • Grievance redressal

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure infrastructure on AWS with regular backups
