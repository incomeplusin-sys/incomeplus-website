What is Volume Pattern Scanner?

Volume Pattern Scanner is a technical analysis tools platform that detects V and U volume patterns in Indian stocks. It helps traders and analysts identify potential market movements based on volume analysis.

How does the scanner work?

The scanner analyzes volume data from Yahoo Finance API using proprietary algorithms to detect specific patterns:

  • V Pattern: 5-candle pattern showing volume decrease then sharp increase
  • U Pattern: 6-candle pattern showing gradual volume accumulation

Each pattern receives a confidence score based on pattern strength and volume characteristics.

How accurate are the pattern detections?

Pattern accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Market conditions: More accurate in trending markets
  • Volume characteristics: Higher volume stocks show clearer patterns
  • Timeframe: Different timeframes may show different patterns
  • Pattern strength: Measured by confidence scores (60-95%)

Important: No technical analysis tool is 100% accurate. Use patterns as one of many analysis tools.
