Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Incomeplus Scanner ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone)
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information (processed by secure gateways)
  • Profile information (company, experience level)

Usage Information:

  • Scan history and results
  • Scanner preferences and presets
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Personalize your experience
  • Analyze usage patterns for improvement
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting services, analytics providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data backup and recovery systems

However, no online service is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and protect your login credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Until account deletion
  • Scan results: 1 year for paid users, 30 days for trial users
  • Payment records: 7 years for tax purposes
  • Activity logs: 6 months for security monitoring

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent

Contact us at privacy@volumescanner.in to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering preferences
  • Analyzing usage patterns
  • Improving service performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Payment Processing: Razorpay, Stripe
  • Analytics: Google Analytics
  • Email Service: SendGrid, Mailchimp
  • Hosting: AWS, DigitalOcean

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in India and other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure adequate protection through contracts and safeguards.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. We will notify users of material changes.
